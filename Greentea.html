Green Tea



Green tea is made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant

that have not undergone the same withering and oxidation process as black and oolong teas.

It has a grassy and slightly bitter flavor, with a light yellow-green color when brewed.

It has high antioxidant content, including catechins and epigallocatechin gallate,

which are believed to have a range of health benefits
