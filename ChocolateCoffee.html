Cholate Coffee

Chocolate Coffee is made by a rich and creamy texture, add chocolate syrup, cocoa powder,

or chocolate chips to brewed coffee before adding milk or cream.

Chocolate coffee recipes may also include other ingredients, such as vanilla extract, cinnamon,

or whipped cream.
